About Me

Hello! My name is Cody Michaud and I was born and raised in the Snohomish area. Currently, I live with my girlfriend and our dog in Cheney, Washington. About a year ago, I graduated from a local Community College with an Associate's Degree in Heavy Equipment and Diesel repair. I worked in that industry for about three years before I got laid off due to Covid. I realized I wanted to change my career path, as I was seeing how much I was destroying my body being in the field I was. I've always been interested in coding and website development, which is why I decided to go back to school to pursue this. Some of my hobbies include working on my car, spending time with my family, and traveling. My goals after this program are to work in the industry for a few yeras as a website developer, maybe even open up my own business and focus on maintaining smaller businesses websites and shops. I would also love to specialize in Shopify and ShipStation as well to broaden my knowledge.
